## Key Takeaways

1. **Genetics & Evolution unit** (Ch 4, 5, 6 of Class 12) is the highest-yield unit = ~13.2 Qs/yr
2. **6 high-priority chapters** contribute ~24 Qs/yr (27% of Biology)
3. **Molecular Basis of Inheritance** alone = 5.6 Qs/yr — the single most important chapter
4. **Class 11**: 21 chapters in syllabus | **Class 12**: 13 chapters in syllabus
5. **3 chapters removed** from rationalized NCERT: Digestion & Absorption (11), Strategies for Enhancement in Food Production (12), Environmental Issues (12)
6. **NTA Unit structure** has 10 units — 5 from Class 11, 5 from Class 12
