## Key Observations

1. **Top 11 chapters** contribute ~49% of all Biology questions.
2. **Molecular Basis of Inheritance** is the single highest-yield chapter (5.9 Qs/yr).
3. **Class 11** has more chapters (19) but Class 12 chapters are denser per chapter.
4. **Genetics & Evolution** + **Human Physiology** together = ~30% of all Biology marks.
5. Recent trend (2025-26): Shift toward multi-fact, statement-based, and match-the-column questions. Assertion-reason eliminated.
